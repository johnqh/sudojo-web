Hidden Pair

Hidden Pair is an advanced technique to remove candidates from certain cells. Pencilmark is required.

In a House (Row, Column, or Block), when two candidates exist only in two cells, you can remove other candidates from those cells.

Let's look at the example:

In Row 9, candidates 5 and 8 can only exist in R9C4 and R9C9.

If R9C4 is 5, then R9C9 is 8. If R9C4 is 8, then R9C9 is 5.

You can remove candidates 3, 4, 6, and 7 from R9C9.
