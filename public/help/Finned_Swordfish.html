Finned Swordfish

Finned Swordfish is a Swordfish with a fin, and you can remove the candidates which are both visible to the fin and Swordfish.

To understand the fin, please see Finned X-Wing.

Let's look at the following example:

 

Column 1, 8, 9 would form a Swordfish for candidate 8, if R9C8 is not 8.

Thus, either Column 1, 8, 9 is a Swordfish, or R9C8 is 8. In either case, R7C7 cannot be 8.
