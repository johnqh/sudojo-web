XYZ-Wing

XYZ-Wing is a variation of XY-Wing.

Rather than looking for a XZ-XY-YZ pattern, the second contains XYZ, so it would be XZ-XYZ-YZ pattern, where XZ and XYZ share a house, XYZ and YZ share a house.

Then, you can elimiate Z from any cells which are visible to all three cells in the XYZ-Wing.
